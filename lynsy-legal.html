Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how LinSy collects, uses, stores, and protects data when you interact with the bot on Discord. We are committed to handling your information responsibly and transparently.

02
Data We Collect

LinSy collects only the data necessary to provide its event management functionality:

  • Discord User IDs — to identify event creators, participants, and administrators
  • Discord Guild (Server) IDs — to associate events and settings with the correct server
  • Discord Channel IDs — to post event cards and schedule feeds in the right channels
  • Discord Role IDs — to configure event roles (e.g. Tank, Healer, DPS) and announcement roles
  • Discord Message IDs — to manage, edit, and delete event cards
  • Event data — title, description, date/time, participant list, comments (if provided by users)
  • Server configuration — language preference, timezone, channel settings, Premium status
We do not collect email addresses, real names, passwords, payment information, or any data outside of Discord.
03
How We Use the Data

Collected data is used exclusively to:

  • Create, display, edit, and delete events
  • Track and manage event signups and participant lists
  • Send pre-event alerts and DM summaries (/me command)
  • Apply server-specific settings (language, timezone, channel roles)
  • Provide event logs to server administrators on request
  • Verify Premium status and apply promo codes

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising or commercial purposes.

04
Data Retention

Event data is stored for as long as the event exists or the bot remains active on the server. Server configuration data persists until the bot is removed or reset.

Server event logs are available for the last 48 hours via the /set_server action:logs command.

When a server removes the bot, its associated data may be deleted after a reasonable grace period.

05
Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties, except:

  • Discord — all bot interactions happen within the Discord platform and are subject to Discord's Privacy Policy
  • Google Calendar — if you use the calendar integration, event data (title, date/time) may be sent to the Google Calendar API to create calendar entries. This is subject to Google's Privacy Policy
06
Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect stored data against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

07
Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data associated with a specific server
  • Ask what data we hold related to your Discord ID
  • Remove the bot from your server at any time, which stops further data collection

To exercise these rights, contact us via the support server.
